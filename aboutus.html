We know how important your pet’s health and wellness is. How many times have you wished that your dog could tell you how he’s feeling? As fellow pet parents, we understand this. ALNpet was born from our desire to improve human-to-pet communication to ensure their health and wellbeing.

We combined our deep technological expertise with a love of animals to build The Ultimate Happy Pet System. Our product, built with state-of-the-art, intelligent technology, was designed specifically to improve communication between you and your pet, enabling you to better understand their physical and emotional needs. Finally, you and your pet can “speak the same language.”
